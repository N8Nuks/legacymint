LegacyMint Safety Online

LegacyMint is being built for athletes, clubs, families and supporters who may be new to crypto. Safety, trust and clear information are core parts of the platform.

Safety First

Never Share Seed Phrases

LegacyMint will never ask for your recovery phrase, private key or password.

Use Official Links

Only mint through official LegacyMint pages and verified collection links.

Check Before Minting

Confirm the NFT image, price, collection name and wallet transaction before approving.

Verified Collections

Official athlete, club and creator collections will be clearly marked as verified.

Beware of fake DMs, copycat collections, fake support accounts and links claiming to offer free NFTs. If unsure, stop and verify before connecting your wallet.
