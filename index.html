LegacyMint Foundation

The LegacyMint Foundation exists to support Youth and Elite Athletes through Community-powered NFT Fundraising.

Funds raised through Genesis Collections, Supporter NFTs and Premium drops will contribute toward:

  • Tournament travel assistance
  • Youth Athlete development
  • Elite competition support
  • Uniforms and equipment
  • Community sports initiatives

Funding distributions will operate through milestone-based pools and Athlete application systems.
